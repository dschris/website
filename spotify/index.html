Chris, Why Did You Remake Spotify in the Web?

I didn't, I promise. Making playlists has always been a hobby of mine, and Spotify has only made it easier (and infinitely more addicting). When someone wants a playlist, it's easy enough to give them a link over text or Facebook to let them follow it, but when someone is interested in more than one it gets a little sloppy. Linking someone 3 or 4 playlists in a row starts to get silly, and people are inevitably trying to recall which one had what music.

So here's my solution. Instead of linking individiual playlists and explaining what was in each one; I've done it once, here. Now I can just link people here and refer to the playlists by name. And it'll also give people a chance to check out some of the other stuff I've done. Thanks to everyone who's given me such positive feedback on these, it makes it feel like a very rewarding hobby to have people listening to your stuff.
